Here is a sample application for leave from school:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Grade]
[Your Roll Number]
[Date]

Respected [Teacher's Name/Principal's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from school for [number] days, starting from [start date] to [end date].

The reason for my absence is [state the reason for leave, e.g. personal reasons, family function, illness, etc.]. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as possible.

I would be grateful if you could grant me permission for this leave. Please let me know if there are any issues or if you need any further information from me.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that this is a sample and you should customize it according to your specific situation and needs. Also, make sure to get the application signed by your parent/guardian if required by your school.
