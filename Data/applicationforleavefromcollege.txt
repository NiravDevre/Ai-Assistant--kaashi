Here is a sample application for leave from college:

**Application for Leave**

[Your Name]
[Your Roll Number/Student ID]
[Your Course/Branch]
[Date]

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to request a leave of [number] days from [start date] to [end date] due to [reason for leave].

I am facing [briefly mention the reason for leave, e.g. personal reasons, family emergency, illness, etc.]. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as possible.

I will ensure that my assignments and projects are completed and submitted on time, and I will also keep up with the coursework during my leave.

I would be grateful if you could grant me leave for the aforementioned period. If there is any issue or concern, please let me know and I will be happy to discuss it with you.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Note: You can modify the above template as per your specific needs and requirements. Also, be sure to check with your college's leave policy and procedures before submitting the application.
